# Teller Step-by-Step Guide
> This is your only guide. Follow exactly in order. Do not skip steps.

---

## ✅ WHAT YOU NEED TO DO WHEN YOU ARRIVE AT WORK

Do these **in this exact order** before serving any customers:

1.  **Login**
    - Enter your username and password
    - Enter your MFA code from your phone
    - ❌ Common mistake: Do not let anyone else use your login. Ever.

2.  **Open your assigned counter**
    1.  Click `Counters` on the left menu
    2.  Find your counter number (ask manager if you don't know which one)
    3.  Click the green `Open Counter` button
    4.  Count every note in your till, one currency at a time
    5.  Type the exact amounts into the boxes
    6.  Double check every number. Click `Confirm Open`

    ❌ Common mistakes:
    - Don't guess the amounts. Count every note.
    - Don't open someone else's counter.
    - If the numbers don't match the expected amount, call your manager **before** confirming.

    📝 What you must write down:
    > Write the opening total amount on your shift sheet before you proceed.

3.  **Check dashboard**
    - The big green box should say `Counter OPEN`
    - If it says anything else, stop and call manager.

---

## 📝 DURING YOUR SHIFT

Do these for every customer:

### When a customer wants to change money:

1.  **Always start with this**:
    > Click `New Transaction` on the dashboard

2.  **Select transaction type**:
    - ✅ **BUY**: You are BUYING foreign currency FROM the customer (they give you foreign notes, you give them MYR)
    - ✅ **SELL**: You are SELLING foreign currency TO the customer (they give you MYR, you give them foreign notes)
    ❌ Common mistake: Selecting the wrong type here will break all your numbers at closing time. Double check this every single time.

3.  **Enter amounts**:
    1.  Select the currency from the drop down list
    2.  Type the amount the customer gave you
    3.  The system will calculate the other amount automatically
    4.  **Count the money in your hand before you click next**

    ❌ Common mistake: Never change the amount after you have counted the customer's money.

4.  **Customer ID check**:
    - If amount is **RM 3,000 or more**:
      ✅ Ask for their IC card
      ✅ Scan or type their IC number
      ✅ Write their full name correctly
    - If amount is **RM 10,000 or more**:
      ✅ You MUST see the original IC. No photos. No copies.
    ❌ Common mistake: Do not process transactions over RM 3000 without ID. You will get in trouble with Bank Negara.

5.  **Final check**:
    - Verify both amounts on screen match what you are holding
    - Verify customer name/IC are correct
    - Click `Process Transaction`
    - Print two receipts: one for customer, one for your till

---

## ⚠️ WHEN YOU SEE THESE MESSAGES

| Error Message | What you do |
|---|---|
| `Waiting for manager approval` | Stop. Do not give any money to the customer. Call your manager. They will approve this transaction. |
| `Insufficient stock` | You do not have enough of this currency in your till. Tell the customer you cannot do this transaction. Call manager if they need this amount. |
| `Customer on sanction list` | Do not say anything to the customer. Cancel the transaction politely. Immediately call your manager in private. |
| `Counter not open` | Go back to Counters page and open your counter first. |
| `Invalid IC number` | Check the number again. If it is correct, call manager. |

---

## 🔄 HANDING OVER YOUR SHIFT

If another teller is taking over your counter:

1.  Finish all pending transactions first. Do not start new ones.
2.  Click `Counters` > `Handover`
3.  Both you AND the incoming teller must be standing at the counter
4.  Count every note together
5.  Enter the amounts. Both of you must verify every number.
6.  You enter your password first. Then they enter their password.
7.  You are no longer responsible for this counter.

❌ Common mistake: Never leave your counter until the handover is 100% complete and confirmed on screen.

---

## ✅ WHAT YOU NEED TO DO BEFORE YOU LEAVE

Do these **in this exact order** before you go home:

1.  **Stop taking new customers 15 minutes before closing time**

2.  **Close your counter**:
    1.  Click `Counters` > your counter > `Close Counter`
    2.  Count **every single note** in your till. One at a time.
    3.  Type the exact amounts into each box.
    4.  Double check every number twice.
    5.  Click `Confirm Close`

    📝 What you must write down:
    > Write every closing amount on your shift sheet. Sign it. Give one copy to manager. Keep one copy for yourself.

3.  **Run your daily report**:
    1.  Go to `Reports` > `Today's Transactions`
    2.  Check that every transaction you did is on this list
    3.  Print this report. Attach it to your shift sheet.

4.  **Final check**:
    - The dashboard should say `Counter CLOSED`
    - Log out
    - Lock your till drawer
    - Hand keys to manager

❌ Common mistakes:
- Do not leave before your counter is fully closed and confirmed on screen.
- Do not ask someone else to close your counter for you.
- Do not guess the closing amounts. Count everything.

---

## ❌ COMMON MISTAKES THAT WILL GET YOU IN TROUBLE

1.  ❌ Do not process a transaction before you have counted the customer's money
2.  ❌ Do not open your counter before counting the opening float
3.  ❌ Do not close your counter without counting every note
4.  ❌ Do not give any money to a customer before the transaction says `COMPLETED`
5.  ❌ Do not share your password or MFA code with anyone. Not even your manager.
6.  ❌ Do not delete or change anything. Every action is logged forever.
7.  ❌ If you make a mistake: **STOP**. Call your manager immediately. Do not try to fix it yourself.

---

> If you are ever unsure about anything: Stop. Call your manager. That is always the correct answer.
